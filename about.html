About · 关于

以石为媒，以刀为笔

Stone as medium, chisel as pen

赵彦青，1987年生于山东，2013年毕业于四川美术学院雕塑系雕塑艺术专业。现为国家一级工艺美术品设计师、四川省工艺美术大师、穆坪砚制作技艺市级非物质文化遗产代表性传承人。

十七年雕石，十七年行走。从川美雕塑系的课堂，到雅安宝兴的汉白玉矿脉；从城市广场上的巨型不锈钢锻造，到掌心方寸间的穆坪砚台——他的创作横跨城市公共艺术与工艺美术两个维度。

2016年，创立彦青雕塑艺术，总部设于重庆，于雅安宝兴设立工艺美术创作基地。城市雕塑作品分布于重庆、四川、贵州、湖北四地，代表作品《诗与远方的归途》矗立于雅安川藏零公里自驾大本营。

他尊重材料原始的脾气，从自然取材。大理石、鹅卵石、陨石、锈铁、船木、工字钢——在他手中，异质的材料彼此对话，剥离外皮，呈现内在的棱角。

"不像外表那么圆滑，内心依然有棱有角。"

Zhao Yanqing (b.1987, Shandong) graduated from the Sculpture Department of the Sichuan Fine Arts Institute in 2013. He is a National First-Class Designer of Arts and Crafts, a Master of Arts and Crafts of Sichuan Province, and a municipal-level bearer of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Muping Inkstone craftsmanship.

Seventeen years carving stone. Seventeen years on the road. From the studios of the Sichuan Fine Arts Institute to the white marble veins of Baoxing, Ya'an; from monumental stainless steel constructions standing in city squares to inkstones held within a single palm — his practice spans the public and the intimate.

In 2016, he founded Yanqing Sculpture Art, headquartered in Chongqing, with a studio dedicated to fine craft in Baoxing. His public sculptures stand across Chongqing, Sichuan, Guizhou, and Hubei — among them The Journey Home, marking the Sichuan-Tibet Zero-Kilometer Self-Drive Base Camp in Ya'an.

He honors the temper of his materials, drawing from nature. Marble, river stones, meteorites, rusted iron, driftwood, structural steel — disparate elements enter into dialogue under his hand, their surfaces stripped back to reveal the angles within.

"Not as smooth on the surface as it seems — the heart still holds its edges."
